About Pixora

Pixora is a collection of fast, simple, and privacy-focused image tools designed to work entirely inside your browser.

Our goal is to make everyday image tasks — such as compressing, resizing, converting, or exporting files — quick and accessible without forcing users to upload their files to external servers.

Privacy-First by Design

Pixora processes images locally on your device using modern browser technologies. Your files are never uploaded, stored, or shared with anyone. What you work on stays on your device.

Why Pixora Exists

Many online tools require uploads, accounts, or unnecessary permissions. Pixora was built as a lightweight alternative — no sign-ups, no tracking, and no hidden data collection.

Whether you are a student, developer, designer, or everyday user, Pixora aims to provide reliable tools that respect your privacy.
