Remove Image Metadata (EXIF)

Remove hidden metadata like location and device info from images. Fast, private, and 100% client-side.

Why remove image metadata?

Photos often contain hidden information such as GPS location, device model, camera settings, and timestamps. This data is called EXIF metadata. Removing it protects your privacy before sharing images online.

How Pixora removes EXIF data

Pixora processes images entirely in your browser using canvas technology. When the image is re-exported, all metadata is automatically stripped. Your image is never uploaded to any server.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool really offline?
Yes. Everything happens on your device. No uploads, no tracking.

Will image quality change?
No visible quality loss. Only hidden metadata is removed.

Does it remove GPS location?
Yes. All EXIF data including location is completely removed.
